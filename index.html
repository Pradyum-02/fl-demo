Our Story

About Sweet trEat

Sweet trEat is a trusted destination in Pashan, Pune known for delicious Indian sweets, hygienic food preparation and flavorful chaats.

From crispy pani puri to mouth-watering sweets and namkeen, every item is prepared with quality and care. Proudly women-owned and family-friendly, we bring the true essence of traditional Indian flavours to your plate — every single day.
